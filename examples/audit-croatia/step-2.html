In the first form group with title 'Pretraživanje po podacima o čestici' you should see:

  • Empty 'Katastarski ured' field with a search option
  • Empty 'Katastarska općina' field with a search option
  • Two fields in the row titled 'Broj kat. čestice': one containing the word 'Broj' and the other containing the word 'Podbroj' next to the 'Traži' button

Below the first form group there should be another form group titled 'Pretraživanje po podacima o posjedovnom listu' which should contain one field:

'Broj posjedovnog lista' which should be empty and next to the 'Traži' button.

Below the two form groups there should be two tables.
