The value you should input into the 'Traži' field is either the municipality name:

{cadastralMunicipalityName}

OR the municipality number:

{cadastralMunicipalityNumber}

but not both at once.

After entering the value, press on the magnifying glass next to the 'Traži' field. If everything went as expected, you should now see some value in the 'Katastarska općina' field.

NOTE: The value you entered may be a bit different from the one you got back. This is fine as long as the municipality number matches the requested search value: {cadastralMunicipalityNumber}.
